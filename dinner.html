Spicy Avocado Melon Tacos

Result

Ingredients

The following amounts are for 4 persons estimated

  • 500g hakket oksekød
  • 4 tsk taco krydderi
  • 2 stk moden avocado
  • 1 spsk citronsaft
  • 0.5 stk honning melon
  • 2 stk forårsløg
  • 1 stk løg
  • 4 stk tortillawraps
  • 150g fromage frais
  • 1 spsk olivenolie

Instructions

  1. Cook the meat in some oil together with the taco spices
  2. Cut the avocado in dices and add lemon juice
  3. Cut the melons in dizes with same sizes as the avocado
  4. Carefully mix the avocado dizes with the melon dizes. Mix together with the cut springonions and red onions
  5. Warm the tortillaes, fold and fill with the spiced meat, salsa and creme fraiche

Comments

I felt like this recipe was pretty good. It was very easy and fast to make and required less stuff to prepare than the usual taco. It tastes pretty good and is a good alternative taco recipe that you can prepare on the days where you want something fast and tasty.

Overall i'll give it 3 out of 5 stars. As simple as it is to make; as simple it tastes. I would prefer the ordinary taco recipe 4 out of 5 times.

Chicken with root vegetables

Result

Ingredients

The following amounts are for 4 persons estimated

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 4-5 stk hvidløg
  • 2 tsk oregano
  • 1 tsk salt
  • 400g root vegetables
  • 500g potatoes
  • 1 tsk peber

Instructions

  1. Cut up the chicken into the usual pieces
  2. Rub in 2 mashed garlic, oregano, salt & pepper
  3. Put the chicken pieces down into a roasting pan together with the potatoes and root vegetables slized into smaller pieces
  4. Add some garlic and springle over some olive oil
  5. Cook for about 45 minutes at 200 degrees celcius.

Comments

This was actually the first time i used a whole chicken for anything. You can really taste the difference and its also cheaper! This dish is very tasty, next time i'll probably serve a green salat together with it. When everything in the dish comes out of the oven the colors can get very monotone so a green salad can balance that out aswell as bring some freshness to the dish.

Overall i'll give it 4 out of 5 stars.

Honey marinated salmon

Result

Ingredients

The following amounts are estimated for 4 persons.

  • 4 stk laksesteaks
  • 2 spsk soyasauce
  • 2 spsk honning
  • 1 stk lime
  • 1 knsp sort peber
  • 1 spsk rapsolie
  • 1 stk rødløg
  • 2 stk hvidløg
  • 300g frisk pasta
  • 500g friske grønne asparges

Instructions

  1. Mix soyasauce, honey, limejuice, pebber and oliveoil
  2. Slize the onions and turn them in the marinade
  3. Turn the salmon in the marinade
  4. Cook the salmon in a preheated 225 degrees oven for 10 to 12 minutes
  5. Cook the pasta according to package instructions
  6. Cook the asparages for about 1-2 minutes in pan boiling water
  7. Serve everything together on a plate

Comments

This recipe had everything. Was easy to make, tasty, healthy and it looks very good. Not really much to comment on, i would serve this dish exactly the same way when i'll make it again.

Overall i'll give it 5 out of 5 stars.

Tortillini with Broccoli & Bacon

Result

Ingredients

The following amounts are estimated for 4 persons.

  • 2 packages w. torellini
  • 400g bacon in dizes
  • 2.5 dl cream
  • 1 stk frisk basilikum
  • 1 knsp muskatnød
  • 1 knsp salt
  • 75g revet mozzarella
  • 2 spsk rasp

Instructions

  1. Boil the tortellini's according to package instructions 'al dente'
  2. Pan roast the bacon dizes and set them aside on some paper to suck out grease
  3. Cut the brocolli og boil very lightly
  4. Bring the cream & basils to a boil. Add spices og poor over the tortellini
  5. Put everything into a roasting pan, springle over mozarella & breadcrumbs
  6. Cook for about 10 minutes at 225 degrees preheated.

Comments

This dish was very easy to make, however the taste wasn't very inspiring. Maybe the quality of the tortellini's can bring it up, but atleast with the supermarket tortellini's you can buy in Denmark this dish simply lacks flavor.

Overall i'll give it 2 out of 5 stars. The taste was just to monotome. If i want something in this alley i'll make lasagne.

Chickenbreast with Oranges & Coriander sauce

Result

Ingredients

The following amounts are estimated for 4 persons.

  • 5 stk korianderfrø
  • 1 tsk kommenfrø
  • 1 tsk chilipeber
  • 450g kyllingebryst
  • 3 stk skalotteløg
  • 1 stk gulerødder
  • 5g ingefær
  • 1 stk fennikelknold
  • 3 stk appelsin
  • 1 stk koriander
  • 2 spsk hasselnøddeoile
  • 1 knsp salt
  • 1 knsp sort peber

Instructions

  1. Mix the spices in a mortar & rub the chicken with it, let it rest for 20 minutes
  2. Cook the chicken with oil at medium heat, 3 minutes each side.
  3. Move the chicken to the side, cover them up.
  4. Onions, carrots, ginger & fenikkel should be washed or cut into smaller pieces.
  5. Cook the cut vegetables, add the jest & juice from the oranges and let the liquid reduce by about 1/3.
  6. Add the chicken breasts and let it simmer for about 6 more minutes

Comments

We went with a more simple version of this dish using fresh koriander and dried kommen aswell as just standard oliveoil instead of the hasselnutoil. We found that we needed to add some orange juice on top of the 3 oranges mentioned in the recipe or else it would have been like a vegetable mush instead of a sauce. So a good advice is to keep some orange juice to add until you find the right balance. The dish was actually suprisingly tasty, next time i'll cook some rice to go with it.

Overall i'll give it 3 out of 5 stars. If there were rice together with it it could probably go up to a 4 out of 5.
